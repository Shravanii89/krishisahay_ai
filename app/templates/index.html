Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Quick answers to common questions about agricultural support schemes

Under PM-KISAN, small and marginal landholding farmer families having cultivable land up to 2 hectares are eligible for income support of ₹6,000 per year, paid in three equal installments. (Note: State exclusions apply).

PMFBY is a crop insurance scheme. It provides insurance coverage and financial support to farmers in the event of failure of any of the notified crops as a result of natural calamities, pests & diseases.

You can upload photos of your Aadhaar card or Land record. Our backend includes a mock AI service replicating Gemini Vision that verifies details and logs them directly to check if they match your profile parameters.
